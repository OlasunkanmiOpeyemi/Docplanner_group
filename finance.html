Finance


The goal of costomers success is making doctors happy and
successful. CS team is a real partner for our costomers and knows
the answers to all difficult questions about our product.

Administration


Infact we have two sales department: inbound and outbound. The
inbound reaches the doctors via phone and convince them
(without any script - we are not a call centre!) to become our
costomers. The outbound team visits doctors in their offices and
tries to make a deal. So if you can sell dust in the desert - join the
team.

User Care


Our user care team is responsible for implementing an order on
our sites. They take care of users opinion about doctors, because
relieable and valuable comments worth their weight in gold.

Sales Operations
